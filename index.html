Definitions

Media
is the material and tools used by an artist, composer or designer to create a work of art
Abstract
art that does not attempt to represent external reality, but seeks to achieve its effect using shapes, forms, colors, and textures
Contemporary Art
In its most basic sense, the term contemporary art refers to art—namely, painting, sculpture, photography, installation, performance, and video art—produced today
Organic Art
is an art form that emphasizes an object alive in its own right and not contrived
Gemoetric Art
is created using geometric elements, and shapes
